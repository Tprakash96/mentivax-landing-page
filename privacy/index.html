Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mentivax (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects and uses information when you visit mentivax.com or contact us. We keep this simple, because we only collect what we actually need to talk to you.

1. Who we are

Mentivax is a school management software provider based in India. This website is an informational site where schools can learn about Mentivax and get in touch with us.

2. Information we collect

We collect only two kinds of information:

  • Information you give us. When you contact us via WhatsApp or email — or send an enquiry — we receive your name, phone number, email address and whatever details you choose to share about your school.
  • Analytics data. Like most websites, we use Google Analytics, which automatically collects standard technical data such as your IP address (typically anonymised), browser and device type, pages viewed and time on site. This is done through cookies and similar technologies.

We do not collect student records, fee data or any school data through this website. Any data handled during a pilot is governed by a separate agreement made directly with your school.

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and arrange demos or free pilots.
  • To understand how our website is used, so we can improve it.
  • To communicate with you about Mentivax, only where you’ve reached out to us.

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to anyone.

4. Cookies & analytics

We use cookies only for Google Analytics, to measure website traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, or install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Blocking cookies will not stop you from using the site.

5. Who we share it with

We only share information with the service providers that make our website and communication work — for example Google (Analytics) and WhatsApp/Meta (when you choose to message us). We may also disclose information if required by law. These providers process data under their own privacy policies.

6. How long we keep it

We keep enquiry details only as long as needed to respond to you and for reasonable follow-up. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics’ standard retention settings. You can ask us to delete your information at any time.

7. Your rights

Under India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, request corrections, request deletion, and raise a grievance. To exercise any of these, email us at tprakash11121996@gmail.com and we’ll respond promptly.

8. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Third-party links

Our site may link to external websites (such as our social media pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

10. Children’s privacy

This website is intended for school administrators and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this site.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest version.

12. Contact us

For any privacy questions or data requests, email tprakash11121996@gmail.com or reach us on WhatsApp.
