What is Mentivax?

An AI assistant that runs
your school — so you don't have to.

Mentivax isn't another school ERP with 50 features you'll never use. It's a single, intelligent assistant that understands what you need when you type it in plain words.

The short version

Mentivax is AI-powered school management software. You type what you need — "how much fee is pending?", "which students are weak in Maths?", "send reminder to parents" — and it gets done. No menus, no training, no clicking through 10 screens.

It replaces the messy Excel sheets, manual registers, and complex software that schools struggle with — and gives every staff member, teacher, and parent the information they need, when they need it.

Why we built this

We saw the same problem everywhere — schools drowning in manual work that software was supposed to fix.

A local school came to us. They were managing all their fees in an Excel sheet — one file, one person who understood it. Errors, missing entries, wrong receipts. They wanted something simple: just track fees digitally.

The interesting part? They had already tried school software before. A big product with dozens of features including fee management. They stopped using it. It didn't reduce their work — it added more. Too many menus, too complex, staff went back to Excel.

We looked around. This wasn't one school's problem. Thousands of small and medium schools — especially in towns and villages — still run on notebooks and spreadsheets. The software that exists is built for big schools with IT teams. It doesn't fit the reality of a 200-student school with 3 office staff.

That's why we built Mentivax.

How it's different

01

AI-first, not menu-first

Other software gives you 50 menus and says "figure it out." Mentivax gives you one text box. Type what you need in plain words — the AI understands and does it. If your staff can send a WhatsApp message, they can use Mentivax.

02

Built for YOUR school, not every school

Mentivax isn't a copy of another product. We don't dump 50 features on you. We start with the one thing that'll save you the most time — fees, attendance, results, whatever hurts most — and build from there. Every school gets exactly what it needs.

03

Reduces work, doesn't add it

This is the test we apply to everything we build: does it save the staff time, or does it create more work? If a feature means more data entry, more clicking, more "filling in forms" — we don't build it. The AI handles the work so humans don't have to.

04

Everyone benefits, not just the office

Most school software is built for the admin. Mentivax is built for everyone — the principal who needs live data, the teacher who wants to know which students are falling behind, the parent who just wants a fee receipt on WhatsApp, and the student who deserves personalised feedback.
