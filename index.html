Honest answers for hesitant schools.

We're not tech people. Is it hard?

There's nothing to learn. If you can type a message, you can use Mentivax. There are no menus to memorise — you just ask, the way you'd ask a colleague.

What happens to our old Excel and registers?

We import all of it for you and clean it up — fees, students, marks. You don't re-type anything, and nothing gets lost in the move.

Is our school's data safe?

Yes. Your data is encrypted, backed up daily, and only your staff can see it. It's far safer than a file sitting on one person's laptop.

Do parents need to install an app?

No. Parents get everything — fee reminders, results, notices — right on WhatsApp, on the phone they already use every day.
