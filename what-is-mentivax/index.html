About Mentivax

What is Mentivax?

The simple idea behind Mentivax — and the four principles we build every feature on.

School management that finally reduces work instead of creating more.

Mentivax is a modern school management platform built around how schools actually work. Fees, records, attendance, reports, parent communication — all in one place, with automation that handles the repetitive work for you.

No 50-screen software that takes months to learn. No spreadsheets that one person controls. Just a clean, simple platform where you type what you need and get it done.

01

Built around how schools actually work.

No complex menus to memorise. Just type what you need in plain words — Mentivax understands and gets it done. Automation works quietly in the background so your staff doesn't have to think about it.

02

Built for YOUR school, not every school

We don't dump 50 features on you. We start with the one thing that'll save you the most time and build from there. Every school gets exactly what it needs.

03

Automates work, doesn't add it

If a feature means more data entry and more clicking — we don't build it. Automation takes care of repetitive work, so your staff can focus on students.

04

Everyone benefits, not just the office

Built for the principal who needs live data, the teacher who wants student insights, the parent who needs a receipt on WhatsApp, and the student who deserves personalised feedback.
