Why Mentivax

Why we built Mentivax.

The story behind Mentivax — and the everyday Excel-and-register problem we set out to fix for small and medium schools.

Because existing software failed the schools that need it most.

A school came to us with a simple request — digitise their fee management. They were running everything in one Excel file. Errors, missing entries, wrong receipts. One person knew how the file worked. When he was absent, the office stopped.

The interesting part? They had already tried school software before. A big product with dozens of features. They stopped using it within months. It didn't reduce their work — it created more. Too many menus, too many screens, staff couldn't keep up.

We looked around. This wasn't one school's problem. Thousands of small and medium schools still run on notebooks and spreadsheets — not because they don't want to go digital, but because the software available doesn't work for them. It's built for big schools with IT teams and full-time operators.

That's why we built Mentivax — software that removes paperwork, not adds more of it. Start with one feature. Add more when ready. No training. No complexity. Just results.
